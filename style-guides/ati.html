background color classes
All BG color classes are slugged with "color-bg-(color or number)"

Ex: color-bg-1 (BU primary color) , color-bg-2 (BU Secondary color)..

Global Spacing classes Padding
Padding & Margins classes follow the Y (top & bottom) and X (right & left) axis.

Ex: p-t-1 padding top 1em , p-b-1 padding bottom 1em, p-y-1 padding top and bottom 1em
Ex: p-r-2 padding right 2em , p-l-2 padding left 2em, p-x-2 padding right & left 2em

Global Spacing classes Margins
Ex: m-r-2 margin right 2em , m-l-2 margin left 2em, m-x-2 margin right & left 2em

Columns

  • NOTE: The column in the framework is 12 grid system, it's a simple math equation that must add up to 12.

    • col-sm-4 + col-sm-4 + col-sm-4 = 3 columns
    • col-sm-3 + col-sm-3 + col-sm-3 + col-sm-3 = 4 columns
    • col-sm-6 + col-sm-6 = 2 columns
    • Column class slugs [xs,sm,md,or lg] all have the same value, they only differ based on breakpoint..
      Example: col-xs-6, col-sm-6, col-md-6, col-lg-6 are all 50%, the difference being at what breakpoint they will turn into 50%.
    • see Media Query section to see when all breakpoint occur
    • See an example 1 column to 2, to 4, to 6 column layout
